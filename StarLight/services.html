Some operators offer their infrastructure for other businesses to run their services. This will help the local business to develop their work.

Prepaid and Postpaid Plans

Offers flexibility in payment methods. Prepaid plans allow users to pay in advance for a set amount of services, while postpaid plans allow users to pay at the end of the billing cycle based on their usage.
